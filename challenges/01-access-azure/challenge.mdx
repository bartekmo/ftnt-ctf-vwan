This is an informational challenge — no scoring, just orientation. Read through it carefully before proceeding.

## Your lab environment

Each team has a dedicated Azure subscription slice pre-provisioned with the following components:

- An **Azure Virtual WAN** with a single hub in **West Europe**
- Two **FortiGate NVA** instances deployed inside the hub (unlicensed at the start)
- A **branch site** simulating an on-premises office — FortiGate + Windows desktop
- A **spoke VNet** that is not yet connected to the hub

Your goal over the course of this workshop is to connect all these pieces together into a working SD-WAN deployment.
