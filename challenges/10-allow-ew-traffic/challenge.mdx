## Allow cross-regional traffic

Every regional spoke server tries to communicate with all other regions. Because of the intent route this traffic is passing your FortiGates and the FortiGates in the other region. Add a firewall policy allowing this traffic (remember about both inbound and outbound connections).

All spoke networks globally fit into a **10.0.0.0/11** space.

#### Verify

Open your spoke web server to see when other regions (besides your own) "go green".
